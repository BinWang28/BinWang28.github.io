## 中文自我介绍

王斌目前是集智进化(MiroMind)的AI研究科学家，专注于大语言模型、多模态AI系统及其实际应用。此前曾任新加坡科技研究局信息通信研究院(I²R, A*STAR)的研究科学家，以及新加坡国立大学的博士后研究员。他于2021年在南加州大学获得博士学位，师从C.C. Jay Kuo教授，2017年于电子科技大学获得工程学士学位。

## Short Bio

Bin Wang is an AI Research Scientist at MiroMind, Singapore. He received his Ph.D. from the University of Southern California, Los Angeles in 2021 and his B.Eng. from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. He was a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore from 2021 to 2023 and an AI scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I²R, A*STAR, Singapore) from 2023 to 2025. His research focuses on multimodal large language models and conversational AI systems. He served as Publication Chair for EMNLP 2023 and as an Editorial Board Member for APSIPA Transactions. He has published over 40 academic papers in top journals and conferences including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ACM KDD, TNNLS, and TASLP, and has received multiple best paper awards.

## About Me

I am currently a research scientist at MiroMind, Singapore, where I focus on large language models, multimodal AI systems, and their practical applications. My previous roles include AI scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I²R, A*STAR) and research fellow at NUS. I earned my Ph.D. from the University of Southern California, Los Angeles, under the supervision of C.C. Jay Kuo. I have a strong track record of academic contributions, with over 40 papers published in top-tier conferences and journals including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ACM KDD, TNNLS, and TASLP. My research has been recognized with multiple best paper awards. I have served as Publication Chair for EMNLP 2023 and as an editorial board member for APSIPA Transactions.
