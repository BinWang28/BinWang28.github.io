Research

Starting from 2017, I entered the field of NLP and language and representation learning, which later became the core technique of AI after years. My early work focused on the intricacies of neural representations, a path that led to significant contributions in ACL, EMNLP, and NAACL.

As the field shifted toward massive pre-trained models, my research pivoted to address the challenges of Multimodal AI, specifically speech and audio signals, integrating linguistic depth with cross-domain data. Today, at MiroMind, I bridge the gap between "the lab" and "the product," applying my doctoral training and my years of research at A*STAR and NUS to solve the next generation of AI agentic challenges in the heart of Singapore's tech ecosystem.
