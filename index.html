About me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at National University of Singapore (NUS) - Human Language Technology (HLT) Lab working with Prof. Haizhou Li.
Before that, I received both Ph.D. and M.S. degrees from University of Southern California (USC) supervised by Prof. C.-C. Jay Kuo in 2021 and 2019, respectively.
My bachelor's degree is obtained from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2017.
