(About Me) I am currently a research scientist at MiroMind, Singapore, where I focus on AGI and its applications. My prior roles include an AI scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R, A*STAR) and a research fellow at the NUS. I earned my Ph.D. from the USC, Los Angeles, under the supervision of C.C. Jay Kuo. I have a strong record of academic contributions, with over 40 papers published in top-tier conferences and journals like ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ACM KDD, TNNLS, and TASLP. My work has been recognized with multiple best paper awards. I have also served as the Publication Chair for EMNLP 2023 and as an editor for APSIPA Transactions.

(Openings@MiroMind) We have opening on Interns (paid) and Full-Time positions at MiroMind. Positions available in Singapore, Beijing, and Shanghai. Interested candidates are welcome to reach out via my email.
