(About Me) I am currently a research scientist at MiroMind, Singapore, where I focus on large language models, multimodal AI systems, and their practical applications. My previous roles include AI scientist at the Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R, A*STAR) and research fellow at NUS. I earned my Ph.D. from the University of Southern California, Los Angeles, under the supervision of C.C. Jay Kuo. I have a strong track record of academic contributions, with over 40 papers published in top-tier conferences and journals including ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ACM KDD, TNNLS, and TASLP. My research has been recognized with multiple best paper awards. I have served as Publication Chair for EMNLP 2023 and as an editorial board member for APSIPA Transactions.
